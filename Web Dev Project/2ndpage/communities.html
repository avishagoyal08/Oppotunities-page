1) Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador

Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors Program is a program to bring together all the students from all over the world who have a passion for technology, have the desire and craze to learn more about technology and help the community. The Ambassadors get an opportunity to interact with their peers, mentors, Professionals, learn various Microsoft technologies, and implement them in the real world.

Eligibility:

  • You must be at least 16 years old, have valid identification and be actively enrolled in an accredited, higher education academic institution.

  • Some experience in coding or technology will definitely be helpful, but do not worry if you don't have much of technical background. Students of all academic backgrounds who are interested in growing their career and tech skills, are encouraged to apply for this program, as all the tools and resources are provided to help you learn more and engage in the program.

2)WomenTech Network

WomenTech Network is a global platform powered by Coding Girls, Tech Family Ventures and Tallocate promoting diversity in tech, leadership and entrepreneurship. The community thrives with the mission to make a global impact and create a difference by enabling women empowerment in tech through leadership development, professional growth, mentorship and networking events for professionals.

Eligibility:
There are no rigid eligibility criteria, any enthusiastic woman engineer can be a part of this community.

3)Women in Tech

Intending to Empower 5 million Women and Girls by 2030, Women in Tech currently has 17 chapters worldwide. They work towards four goals-Education, Entrepreneurialism, Social Inclusion, Science & Innovation. They offer Coding programs, Scholarships, High school programs, Internships, Role Models programs, Corporate programs, Pitching classes, Mentoring, Trade missions, Job opportunities, Incubation, and Acceleration camps. Full support and development programs that seek to introduce digital skills to minorities and underprivileged communities are also a primary function.

Eligibility: Female
